Event Information

Friday, April 10, 2020
10:00am–7:00PM

FREE ADMISSION

In celebration of its 25th Anniversary, George Mason University's Cultural Studies PhD program will host a symposium featuring talks by leading academic researchers, community organizers, activists, and laborers focusing on the cultural, political, and economic impacts of Amazon.

In the wake of Amazon's announcement of HQ2 coming to Arlington, VA, the symposium will be held at George Mason University's Arlington Campus.

Keynote Speakers

OUR KEYNOTE SPEAKERS include internationally renowned scholars who have performed innovative research on Amazon. Stay tuned for more information on their talks and scheduled time slots.
